# Swiggy Refund & Cancellation Policy (Sample)

This document summarizes the refund and cancellation rules that apply to customer orders placed via the Swiggy platform.

## 1. Cancellation Window

1.1 Orders can be cancelled **free of charge** only before the restaurant has accepted the order. The "Cancel" button is available on the order tracking screen during this window.

1.2 Once the restaurant has accepted the order and started preparation, cancellation is generally **not permitted**. In exceptional cases (e.g., extreme delay), support may cancel on your behalf with a partial refund.

1.3 For **Instamart** orders, cancellation is allowed only until a picker has been assigned. After that, cancellation may incur full charges.

## 2. Refund Timelines

2.1 Refunds to the **original payment method** (UPI, card, net banking) are processed within **5–7 business days**.

2.2 Refunds to **Swiggy Money wallet** are instant and can be used on the next order.

2.3 For **Cash on Delivery** orders that were not delivered, refunds are issued to the Swiggy Money wallet by default.

## 3. Eligible Refund Scenarios

You are eligible for a full or partial refund in the following cases:

- Order not delivered (courier lost, address unreachable).
- Wrong item delivered.
- Missing item in the order.
- Food quality issue reported with photo evidence within 1 hour of delivery.
- Packaging spillage affecting majority of the order.
- Significant delay beyond 2x the promised ETA.

## 4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

- Customer-initiated cancellation after restaurant acceptance.
- Subjective taste preferences ("I didn't like the flavor").
- Refusal of delivery at the doorstep for reasons unrelated to order quality.
- Orders delivered correctly as per the menu description.

## 5. How to Request a Refund

5.1 Open the Swiggy app → Orders → select the relevant order → tap **Help**.

5.2 Choose the issue category and upload supporting evidence (photos) where applicable.

5.3 A support agent or the automated system will respond within 30 minutes for most categories.

## 6. Swiggy One Members

Swiggy One members receive **priority refund processing** and extended refund eligibility windows (90 minutes instead of 60 minutes for food quality complaints).

## 7. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may escalate via the in-app chat. Escalations are reviewed by a senior support team within 48 hours.
