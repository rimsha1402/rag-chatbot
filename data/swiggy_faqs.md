# Swiggy Customer FAQs

## Orders

**Q: How do I place an order on Swiggy?**
Open the Swiggy app, allow location access, browse restaurants near you, add items to your cart, choose a delivery address, select a payment method, and tap "Place Order". You will receive live tracking updates until delivery.

**Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?**
You can cancel an order for free only before the restaurant has accepted it. Once accepted, cancellation charges (up to the full order value) may apply because the food is already being prepared. Item modifications are not supported after placing the order — please cancel (if still possible) and re-order.

**Q: What is the minimum order value?**
Minimum order value varies per restaurant and is shown on the restaurant page. It is typically between ₹99 and ₹199. Orders below this minimum cannot be placed.

## Delivery

**Q: How long does delivery usually take?**
Typical delivery time is 25–40 minutes, depending on distance, weather, and restaurant preparation time. The exact ETA is shown before you place the order.

**Q: What is Swiggy Instamart?**
Swiggy Instamart delivers groceries and daily essentials in 15–30 minutes from dark stores in your city. It is a separate section inside the Swiggy app.

**Q: What if my delivery is late?**
If your order is significantly delayed beyond the promised ETA, you may be eligible for a coupon or partial refund. Contact support from the order help section.

## Payments

**Q: What payment methods are supported?**
Swiggy supports UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, Swiggy Money wallet, and Cash on Delivery (COD). COD availability depends on the restaurant and order value.

**Q: I was charged but the order failed. What should I do?**
Failed-order charges are automatically refunded to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. If not received, raise a ticket from Help & Support with your order ID.

## Swiggy One (Membership)

**Q: What is Swiggy One?**
Swiggy One is a paid membership that offers unlimited free delivery on eligible orders above ₹149, extra discounts at partner restaurants, and free delivery on Instamart orders above ₹199.

**Q: How much does Swiggy One cost?**
Pricing varies by city and plan duration. A 3-month plan is typically ₹299 and an annual plan is typically ₹899. Check the app for current pricing.

## Account

**Q: How do I update my delivery address?**
Go to Account → Addresses → Add/Edit Address. You can save Home, Work, and Other addresses.

**Q: How do I delete my Swiggy account?**
Go to Account → Help → Account-related → Delete my account. Deletion is permanent and removes your order history.
